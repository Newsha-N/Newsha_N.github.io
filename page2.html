Bulk Club Member Project

This program supports the Bul Club that includes a full information of all the Executive and Regular customers of the club. The store manager and the administrator each have spacial access to members information such as their personal info, shopping history, membership renewal and expiration and adding new items to shopping item. To give you a full image, it perfectly performs for any supermarkets or any online shopping website. This code has been developed using Qt and SQLite, perfectly shows each item and the buyer. Please take a look at the Activity Diagram and a Short State Diagram of the overal code.
