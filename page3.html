National Parks Project

This program has an interactive pamphlet to provide information for vacationers who want to visit our National parks. The pamphlet includes a full description of the parks including their name and acres and the number of visitors. The client has the ability to manipulate the list to obtain any information they want. Also, the authorized personel can change, add or delete more parks with information specific information for each park. This program is using Graphical User Interface, Qt and SQLite. Please take a look at the UML Diagram and a Short State Diagram of the overal code.
